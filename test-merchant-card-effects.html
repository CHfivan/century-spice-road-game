Size Analysis

Desktop Sizing:

  • Regular cards: 150px × 200px with 24px spice cubes
  • Mini cards: 80px × 120px with 13px spice cubes
  • Size ratio: 13/24 = 54% (matches card width ratio of 80/150 = 53%)

Mobile Sizing (≤768px):

  • Regular spice cubes: 12px
  • Mini card spice cubes: 8px
  • Size ratio: 8/12 = 67% (proportional to mobile card scaling)

Extra Small Screens (≤480px):

  • Regular spice cubes: 18px
  • Mini card spice cubes: 10px
  • Size ratio: 10/18 = 56% (maintains proportion)
